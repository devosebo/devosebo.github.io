Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Short version: your data never leaves your device.

Data storage

All data you enter into Moniq — transactions, categories, budgets, settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

No account required

Moniq does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

No analytics or tracking

Moniq does not include analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. No usage data, behavioral data, or device identifiers are collected.

Third parties

Moniq does not share any data with third parties. There are no integrations with external services that access your financial data.

Your control

You can export your data as a CSV file from within the app at any time. You can delete all app data using the reset option in settings. Uninstalling Moniq removes all associated data from your device.

A note on future iCloud sync

A future update may add optional iCloud sync. If it does, that sync would be directly between your devices and Apple's iCloud — Moniq would never see or store your data on any server operated by us. If this ever changes, this policy will be updated and the date above revised.

Children

Moniq is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
