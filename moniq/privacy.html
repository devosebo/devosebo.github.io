Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how moniq handles your information. The short version: your data never leaves your device.

Data Storage

All data you enter into moniq — including transactions, categories, budgets, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

No Account Required

moniq does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

No Analytics or Tracking

moniq does not include any analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. No usage data, behavioral data, or device identifiers are collected or shared.

Third Parties

moniq does not share any data with third parties. There are no integrations with external services that access your financial data.

Your Control

You have full control over your data at all times:

You can export your data as a CSV file from within the app. You can delete all app data using the reset option in the app's settings. Uninstalling moniq removes all associated data from your device.

Children

moniq is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
